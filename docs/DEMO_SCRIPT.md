# VoiceIQ Demo Script (5-7 Minutes)

## 0) Opening (20-30s)
"Last month, a customer in Surulere sent three WhatsApp messages in one week saying her data keeps cutting out. She mentioned Airtel twice. Nobody flagged it. Nobody called her. She ported. That customer represented N18,000 in annual revenue. Multiply that by 200,000 customers sending the same signals every month — and you understand why we built VoiceIQ."

## 1) Problem framing (30-45s)
- Telcos have data, but retention is reactive.
- Behavioral churn models miss the richest real-time signal: what customers are saying now.
- Nigerian language complexity (Pidgin + code-switching) makes off-the-shelf tools weak.

## 2) Solution overview (45-60s)
- VoiceIQ ingests voice, WhatsApp, and SMS interactions.
- It detects churn intent, frustration, and complaint patterns.
- It combines those signals with behavioral features into an explainable churn score.
- Agents get next-best actions in real time.

## 3) Live demo flow (2-3 mins)
1. Open the Live Agent Dashboard.
2. Play the Chioma scenario: show live transcript with Pidgin and intent signals.
3. Point to detected signals (port intent, repeat contact, competitor mention).
4. Compare churn scores:
   - Behavioral only: 61%
   - VoiceIQ: 87%
5. Show SHAP explanation in plain English.
6. Trigger retention action:
   - Offer 1GB data
   - Escalate to retention team

## 4) Validation proof (45-60s)
- Show the validation card with baseline vs VoiceIQ model metrics.
- Mention lift@10% and false-positive reduction.
- Emphasize that conversational signals produce earlier, more accurate targeting.

## 5) Governance + trust (30s)
- Offer policy prevents over-discounting.
- Explainability makes agent decisions auditable.
- Fallback mode if transcription confidence is low.

## 6) Business impact (30s)
- 1% churn reduction across 80M subscribers = huge revenue protection.
- VoiceIQ captures the signal before the customer ports.

## 7) Close (10-15s)
"The calls are already happening. VoiceIQ makes them count."
